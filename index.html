My Story

I'm a professional with 12.3 years of experience in Information Technology, currently working with Amdocs as a DevOps Engineer. My core expertise spans WebLogic, Tuxedo, Jenkins, Site Reliability Engineering, Unix shell scripting, Bitbucket, Git, and Apache Tomcat — with growing hands-on experience in Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift (OCP), and Kafka.

My career began at Infosys as a Senior Middleware Administrator, where I administered WebLogic and Tomcat servers, handled Tibco integrations, and managed incident and problem resolution for a US-based client. Since December 2018, I've been with Amdocs as a Technology Integration Engineer and DevOps Engineer, supporting a Canada-based client, where I focus on system reliability, major incident management, and change management compliance.

I'm goal-oriented with a strong commitment to troubleshooting, automation, and delivering timely, production-grade solutions.
